Welcome to VOLTZ extras!
Here you can see the different visual and sound creations of VOLTZ, as well as the game mechanics in detail.

MECANICS

A cable connects the players. It allows you to hang on to your teammate and prevents the two players from moving away. Its elasticity will be useful in the platform phases, it's up to you to discover the best way to use it!


When you are on a generator, you are charged. Your teammate receives power through the cable that connects you, and can then activate electrical objects.

CONTROLS

To move, use WASD (jump, go left, crouch, go right). These controls apply to all the characters you control. In other words, in the case of local multiplayer, it allows you to control both characters. In online multiplayer, it allows you to control Yu (the cyan character) if you create the game, or Wu (the pink character) if you join it. In local multiplayer, OKL; will correspond to Yu only while Wu's will be the arrows. A menu to exit the game and change its settings is also available by pressing Escape.
